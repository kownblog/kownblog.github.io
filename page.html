Scratched Diary=Written by Vaibhav Singh Bisht:)

71.Kehte Sab Usko Shayar, Par Woh Likhta Sirf Ek Ke Liye Hai. Uske Pass Shabd Toh Hazaaro Hai, Par Woh Sab Shabd Milte Sirf Ek Ke Liye Hai.

72.Kehte Sab Usko Shayar, Par Woh Likhta Sirf Ek Ke Liye Hai. Uske Pass Shabd Toh Hazaaro Hai, Par Woh Sab Shabd Milte Sirf Ek Ke Liye Hai.

73.Khusiyon Ki Umar Thori Kam Hi Rakhni Chahiye, Aaj Kal Lambi Umar Wale Logo Ko Waise Bhi Pasand Nahi Karte

74.Gammo Ka Naqab Chehre Pe Rakhna Chodh De, Banawati Muskurahat Bhi Itna Banana Chodh De, Chutki Bhari Khushi Ko Pahad Banake Dekhana Chodh De, Aaj Mai Hoon Yaar, Tere Zindagi Ko Sawarne Ke Liye. Kal Kya Karoge Agar Meri Saansien Mera Halak Chodh De.

75.Wafa Me Unke Kashish Thi, Bewafa Hote Hi Nafrat Hogayi Unse.

76.Woh Jo Sabko Suljhati Thi, Khud Me Hi Ulajh Gae. Mushkil Me Akeli Reh Kar, Dil Ki Baatien Dimag Se Kar Gae.

77.Jabtak Tumhe Yeh Baat Yaad Na Rahe, Tabtak Mai Intzaar Karke Dekhta Hoon. Chalo Tum Mujhse Dosti Hi Karlo, Mai Zindagi Bhar Mohabbat Karke Dekhta Hoon.

78.Tere Mere Rishte Ko Be-Naam Hi Rehne Do, Logo Ke Sawaal Aaj Fir Taal Do. Jab Jawaab Hume Maloom Hai, Toh Sabko Batana Zaroori Hai Kya? Kuch Ajeeb Sa Hi Sahi, Kuch Be-Manzil Hi Sahi. Is Rishte Ko Fir Yun Hi Rehne Do.

79.Mai Ghayal Pada Sher, Mera Ghor Shaant Khel. Mera Libas Mera Kafan, Tera Raaz Mujhpe Dafan. Kalyug Hoon Mai, Qayamat Meri Neeti Hai. Bin Haatho Ke Prayog Ke Maine Saarien Jung Jeeti Hai.

80.Ajnabee Rehna Hi Sahi Tha Humare Liye, Kam Se Kam Khamoshi Dard Toh Na Deti.

81.Mujhse Mohabbat Karne Chalna Toh Irade Majboot Rakhna, Kyuki Mujhse Nafrat Karna Asaan Hai.

82.Hum Dono Ke Beech Ab Khamoshi Hi Sahi Lagti Hai. Baatien Kab Jhagdo Me Badal Jaae Pata Hi Nahi Chalta.

83.Bas Tum Chaar Logo Ke Saamne Kehdeti Ho, Shayad Isliye Sahi Lagti Ho Par Acchi Nahi. Bas Mai Chaar Logo Ke Saamne Tumhari Izzat Kar Chup Rehta Hu, Shayad Isliye Mai Galat Lagta Hu Par Bura Nahi.

84.Mujhe Dhoond Lena Agar Kho Dena Waqt Humara, Woh Yaadien Fir Chodh Jaunga, Jab Wapas Aana Parega Firse Dobara. Tumhe Dhoondne Ke Liye Ek Baar Fir Milne Aaunga.

85.Zindagi Us Mukaam Pe Le Aaya Hoon, Ki Ab Mere Liye Meri Saansien Hi Kaafi Hai Khush Rehne Ke Liye.

86.Har Ek Tarfa Aashiq Ki Kahani Ek Jaisi Hoti Hai, Kehte Hai Sab Ki Usse Hi Pyaar Karunga Zindagi Bhar. Par Aant Me Usko Bhi Aage Badhna Padhta Hai.

87.Uska Sawaal Hai Mujhse, Ki Kya Umeed Lekar Intzaar Karte Ho? Mera Jawab Hai Usse, Umeed Hai Nahi Bas Be-Shart Pyaar Karta Hu.

88.Mere Intzaar Ka Imtehaan Mat Lo, Kyuki Is Intehaan Ke Liye Maine Padhai Ki Hai.

89.Woh Kehti Hai Ki Intzaar Kyu Karte Ho? Mai Kehta Hu Ki Tum Us Layak Jo Ho.

90.Tere Bin Mai Kuch Kam Sa Honga, Raat Bin Chand Sa Honga.

91.Kamzor Pada Hu Par Abhi Hara Nahi Hoon. Mitti Pe Pada Hu Par Abhi Mitti Me Mila Nahi Hoon.

92.Gir Ke Uth Aur Dobara Girne Ka Hausla Rakhta Hoon. Mai Manzil Ko Nazro Ke Saamne, Aur Koshisho Ka Hisaab Nahi Rakhta Hoon.

93.Woh Jo Khud Ki Pehchaan Nahi Dhoond Paate Hai, Woh Aksar Dusro Ki Churane Me Lag Jaate Hai.

94.Dard Ko Aankh Me Aakar, Aansu Ban Kar Behtey Dekha Hai. Maine Sapno Ko Haath Me Aakar, Haatho Se Girte Dekha Hai.

95.Likhawat Panne Pe Aur Raaz Dil Me Rakhta Hoon. Burai Mooh Par Aur Bhalai Peet Piche Karta Hoon..

96.Mere Khayal Aur Khwaab Dono Me Aati Hai, Khushi Hotho Se Aur Khabar Aankhon Se De Jati Hai.

98.Samundar Ki Gehrai Woh Apni Aankhon Me Le Aae, Aasman Ki Uchaai Woh Humare Beech Me Le Aae.

99.Kuch Khwaab Rakhta Hoon, Unko Bas Khud Tak Rakhta Hoon. Kuch Sawaal Rakhta Hoon, Unke Jawab Bas Tum Tak Rakhta Hoon.

100.Woh Tujhe Rokna Chahenge, Kyuki Tum Woh Ban Jaoge, Jo Woh Khud Kabhi Ban Nahi Paenge.

101.Chali Jana Door Mujhse, Bhool Jana Mujhe Kuch Dino Ke Liye. Agar Yaad Me Nahi Aaya Toh Zindagi Se Nikal Dena. Agar Dil Me Fir Bhi Raha Toh Ek Mulakkat Mujhse Fir Kar Lena.

102.Woh Parakh Lenge Tujhe, Tarash-na Parega Tujhe Khud Ko. Woh Hara Hua Maan Lenge Tujhe, Heera Bana Na Parega Tujhe Khud Ko.

103.Mere Hathon Ki Lakeeron Ko Mujhse Wafa Manzoor Nahi Thi. Fariyaad Meri Woh Sunli Jo Mujhe Kabhi Qubool Nahi Thi. Fir Bhi Hum Karte Rahe Ishq Bin Soche Ki Manzil Yeh Meri Nahi, Zidd Hoti Toh Shayad Meri Hoti Par Mohabbat Thi, Meri Fitoor Nahi Thi.

104.Un Raaton Ka Hisaab Hai Mere Pass, Jin Raaton Ko Tere Diye Zakhmo Ne Sone Nahi Diya. Par Un Dino Ka Hisaab Nahi Rakh Paya, Jin Dino Me Teri Di Muskurahat Ne Mujhe Gum Ko Choone Nahi Diya.

105.Hawaniyat Karne Wala Insaan Nahi Hota Hai, Dharam Kissi Doshi Ka Nahi Hota Hai. Toh Fir Kyu Us Doshi Ko Bachaya Gaya? Toh Fir Kyu Us Beti Dafnaya Nahi Diya Gaya?

106.Unhe Fikar Tumhari Tab Tak Hai, Jab Tak Cheekh Tumhari Taaza Hai, Unhe Fikar Tumhari Tab Tak Hai, Jabtak Cheekh Me Tumhari Goonj Hai.

107.Woh Marham Lagane Tab Tak Aaenge, Jabtak Ghaav Tumhare Mashoor Hai.

108.Mujhe Teri Yaadon Ki Hirasat Me Band Kar Khud Se Door Kardena, Mujhe Ek Yaad Dekar Usko Zindagi Bhar Yaad Karne Me Majboor Kardena.

109.Uski Rooh Meri Mumkin Hone Ki Iltiza Hai, Meri Aashiqui Meri Saanson Ki Khata Hai. Mujhe Maloom Nahi Maqsad-e-fitoor. Usse Maloom Nahi Apne Aankhon Ka Suroor.

110.Dhoondna Chahunga Usko Ek Manzil Pe Laakar Kho Dene Ke Baad, Mai Usse Mohabbat Dobara Karna Chahunga Fanah Hone Ke Baad.

111.Agar Mulakaatien Nazron Ki Hoti, Toh Shayad Bhool Jata Hazaro Ke Beech.

112.Mulakaatien Jazbaaton Ki Thi, Shayad Isliye Pehchaan Liya Laakho Ke Beech.

113.Humari Aukaat Kya Jo Zindagi Ki Keemat Lagate Hai, Us Haisiyat Ki Chahhat Me Khushiyan Ko Aag Lagate Hai.

114.Nashe Me Gir Jaane Ki Agar Hidayat Hoti, Toh Mai Har Todh Firse Uske Ishq Gir Jata.

115.Hum Unhe Chodh Fir Wapas Chale Jate Hai, Kahin Usse Humari Zaroorat Na Ho, Isliye Hum Woh Bhool Dobara Kar Aate Hai.

116.Kaash Woh Dekh Leti Mere Aankhon Me Khalish. Shayad Mai Bol Deta Fir Jubaan Se Jo Thi Khalal.

117.Dhali Hue Shyaam Ke Saath Aaj Doob Jaane Ka Mann Tha Andhero Me. Jo Log Sambhal Sakte The Mujhe Aaj Toda Bhi Unhone.

118.Mujhe Meri Saanson Ki Parwah Hoti, Toh Usse Milna Chodh Deta. Jab Nazron Me Uski Kashish , Tab Mai Saansh Lena Chodh Deta.

119.Yeh Mera Rasta, Mere Raste-e-Raahi Jhoothe, Aksar Bina Koshish Ke Haarne Wale Log, Koshish Karke Haarne Wale Logo Ko Nicha Dikhane Ki Koshish Karte Hai.

120.Tum Kissi Ki Parchai Bankar Andhere Me Chamak Nahi Paoge, Jab Khud Ko Jalaugey Tab Hi Aag Ban Raat Ko Roshan Kar Paoge.

121.Kissi Ko Gira Ke Koi Khud Ba Khud Aage Nahi Bhadega. Ghadi Ki Sui Ruk Jaane Se Waqt Kabhi Nahi Rukega.

122.Mitti Fek Dena Chahe Uspe Kitni, Usko Zameen Na Bana Paoge. Gir Tere Mooh Pe Aaegi, Aasman Ko Kabhi Dafna Nahi Paoge.

123.Khuda Se Uski Khushi Maangne Ki Ilteja Purani Hai, Ibidat Me Naam Uska, Aur Khuda Se Ladai Purani Hai.

124.Mohabbat Hone Pe Tu Uska Hafiz Na Hua Toh Woh Mohabbat Kaisi. Khamoshiyon Me Uski Zaroorat Na Samjhi Toh Woh Chahaht Kaisi.

125.Mere Rukhsat Hone Pe Mujhe Mukhaalif Kar Dena, Guzaarish Maan Lo Tab Shayad Yaadon Me Rehne Ki Gilaa Nahi Hogi..

128.Sohbat Yaad Rakhna Meri, Gair Hone Pe Bhi Majood Rahunga. Dooriyaan Bas Nazdeekiyon Me Aaengi, Qareeb Rooh Se Hoon Aur Usse Hi Waakif Rahunga.

126.Sapno Ki Zameen Pe Ek Jagah Tumne Bhar Di, Khokla Ek Hissa Karke, Zindagi Se Ek Jagah Khali Kardi.

127.Kuch Musafir Makaan Ko Ghar Kar Dete Hai. Par Hote Toh Woh Musafir Hai Na, Chodh Jaane Ki Niyat Leke Aate Hai, Chodhkar Fir Ghar Ko Makaan Kar Dete Hai.

128.Deewane Toh Tumhare Bahut Hai, Mehfillo Me Charche Tumhare Bahut Hai. Koi Kehta Hai Ki Qatil Ho, Koi Matlabi Keh Deta Hai. Koi Tumhe Mera Kehta, Koi Mera Zikar Tak Nahi Karta Hai. Saazish Hoti Kaash Teri, Kaash Mehkhaano Me Na Hoti Raat Meri. Par Un Mehkhaano Me Naam Tumhara Mashoor Hai, Shayad Koi Humdard Nahi Par Deewane Toh Tumare Bahut Hai.

129.Kyu Mai Jagoon Jab Uske Sapno Pe Haq Kissi Aur Ka Hai, Kehna Ab Baaki Kya? Jab Raaton Me Bujh Jaane Ka Naseeb Mera Hai.

130.Tukdo Me Se Sambhala Hai Khud Ko, Jab Todne Wale Hi Samet Rahe The Mujhe.

131.Intzaar Lamba Ho Jaega Agar Ab Na Mile, Waqt Kaatna Mushkil Hojaega Agar Waqt Pe Nahi Mile, Fursat Shayad Mumkin Nahi Tujhe, Par Fir Shayad Mujhe Fursat Naseeb Na Ho Agar Ab Na Mile.

132.Apni Saanson Ko Meri Taraf Aate Mehsoos Toh Kar, Kinare Tak Pahuchne Ke Liye Kashti Tanyar Toh Kar, Kaagaz Ki Kashti Lehro Me Se Guzar Jaegi. Apni Chahat Ki Hawa Mere Samundar Pe Toh Rakh..

136.Haasil Kya Karoge Hume Nicha Dikha Ke, Sir Jhuka Ke Toh Dekh Rahe Ho Na Tum Hume?

137.Kabhi Qareeb Aakar Mere Aankhon Me Dekh, Wafa Ki Shikayat Toh Kar, Mumkin Hai Ki Mere Waadien Be hadh Dekh, Fanah Hojao.

138.Bas Itni Si Chahat Leke Chalta Hu, Judaai Ko Qismat Aur Na Alag Hone Ki Fariyaad Rakhta Hu.

139.Kehna Bhi Kya, Uljahanien Gher Rakhi Hai Mujhe. Sawarna Bhi Kya, Jab Shikayatien Hi Roki Rakhi Hai Tujhe.

140.Humari Judai Ki Jo Haath Me Lakeer Thi, Maine Kanjhar Se Usko Mitaya Hai.

141.Ab Jo Bin Tumhare Waqt Beetega, Woh Har Pal Jaya Hai.

142.Majboori Ki Hadd Toh Dekh, Pyaas Hai Ek, Aur Paani Bahut Sara, Meri Kismat Ki Hadd Toh Dekh, Hoon Samundar Me Aur Paani Khara.

143.Ek Mohabbat Ka Karz Hai Tumpe Chukane Ki Hasiyat Nahi Tumhari.

144.Iss Karz Ko Utarna Hai Sir Se, Chahe Ab Beet Jae Zindagi Humari.

145.Kya Hai Woh Log Jo Pyaar Ka Sauda Karte Hai, Mehal Me Leja Ke, Ghareeb Khane Ki Baat Karte Hai.

146.Chaand Hai Badalon Me, Aur Falak Suna Suna Lag Raha.

147.Raaton Ki Tanhai Hai, Ab Andhera Bhi Jaane Ki Baat Keh Raha.

148.Kon Ho Tum Aaj Zara Yaad Dila Do, Bhar Gae Hai Woh Ghaav, Aaj Fir Se Taaza Kara Do.

149.Meethi Si Aankhon Me Is Duniya Ka Kadwa Sach Rakhti Hai, Jhooth Hi Lage Woh Aisa Bhi Sach Bolti Hai. Haan Log Usse Aksar Ruth Jaate, Fir Zyada Baat Nahi Karte. Par Niyat Toh Dekho Uski, Woh Aag Khud Me Laga Ke Dusro Ko Andhere Me Nahi Rakhti Hai.

150.Kaisa Hai Yeh Din, Kaash Fir Na Aae. Lachaar Khud Ko Yun Dekh Kar, Khud Pe Taras Kaash Fir Na Aae.

151.Qismat Ko Kos Du? Ya Halat Se Haar Jau? Ab Mushkil Waqt Me Ladai, Khud Se Karu? Ya In Sab Se Upar Uth Jau.

152.Jaana Zaroori Jab Tera Na Ho, Bas Itni Si Chahat Hai. Aana Wapas Pare Tujhe Mere Pass, Ese Ghar Ki Chahat Hai.

153.Tera Ghar Mera Ho, Mere Ghar Ki Sazawat Teri Ho. Khushiyan Teri Aur Muskurahat Meri Ho. Kabhi Hum Itna Nazdeek Aae, Ki Saansien Teri Aur Dhadkan Meri Ho.

154.Mushkil Waqt Me Saath Chodh Diya, Qismat Ke Saath, Unhone Rukh Mod Liya. Waadien The Kabhi Maut Saath Dekhne Ke, Phir Jab Sambhala Nahi Gaya, Toh Haath Chodh Diya

155.Bheegi Hui Bistar Ki Chadhar Pe, Rang Sapno Ka Bikhra Hua. Samet Lunga Gam Lamho Me, Nishaan Lamho Ka Gehra Hua.

156.Kho Gayi Hai Zaroorat, Ya Yaad Rakhi Hi Nahi Kabhi Ehmiyat? Ab Sehna Hai Kya Yeh Zillat, Ya Yaad Krogi Jab Milegi Fursat?

157.Woh Gusse Me Yun Bol Di Ki Karte Ho Mohabbat Jism Se Toh Aaj Karlo Apni Bhookh Puri. Maine Bhi Hayya Se Aankh Choom Kar Kaha Ki Bhookh Hoti Toh Aajtak Na Hoti Tu Meri.

158.Na Woh Meri Mohabbat Pehli Hai, Na Koi Meri Mohabbat Aakhri Hai. Jahan Dil Thera Hai Pal Do Pal Ke Liye, Un Lamho Ki Har Saans Aakhri Hai.

159.Mehfil Me Jo Lafz Pyaar Ke, Bol Diye Jo Yaar Se. Woh HumNashi Na Hue Toh Hum Zaroor Nashe Me Honge.

160.Mujhe Maloom Na Hua Kab Unhe Hum Jhoothe Lagne Lage, Dil Todne Tak Sahi Tha Pata Nahi Kab Unhe Humare Waadien Bebuniyaad Lagne Lage.

170.Pahad Pe Chad Ke Girna, Gir Ke Dobara Khade Uthna. Hausla Pahad Se Bada Hai, Pahad Bhi Bebak Khada Hai. Haar Jana Toh Ab Gir Jaane Se Bura, Haar Jane Ke Liye Gire Hai Rehna. Ab Har Kadam Hai Zaroori, Abhi Toh Kahani Hai Adhoori. Pura Karke Dekhana Hai, Pahad Pe Chad Ke Girna, Gir Ke Uthna Hai.

171.Yakeen Bas Mujhe Meri Chahat Pe Tha, Halat Toh Humesha Se Khilaf The.

172.Bin Tere Jo Jiye Toh Kayal Honge Hum, Mushkil Agar Halat Na Hue, Toh Kya Ishq Karenge Hum.

173.Dard Tha Mera Woh Marham Ban Kar Aae, Woh Zaroorat Kab Ban Gayi Unhe Ehsaas Nahi Hua. Na Unhe Samjh Aaya, Na Maine Uff Kiya. Woh Marham Bankar Aae Par Mujhe Bhi Aaram Nahi Hua.

174.Uski Surat Mere Halat Badal Kar Mujhe Khush Kar Deti Hai, Woh Mujhe Dekh Bimar Ko Bhi KhushNaseeb Samjh Leti Hai.

175.Karti Ho Mohabbat Toh Yun Kabhi Rooth Na Jana, Mujhe Yaad Karti Ho Toh Kabhi Batana Bhool Na Jana. Zaroori Hume Muskarahat Hai Tumhari, In Ashkon Ko Aankhon Me Dabakar Kar Kabhi Na Muskarna.

176.Azaad Soch Ko Rakho, Qaidi Toh Qaid Hone Ka, Khayal Bhi Bana Deta Hai.
